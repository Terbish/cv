KupoBot

Kupo Bot is a FFXIV Helper Discord Bot available for personal use on your Discord server! His goal is to make looking up information quick and painless without ever needing to step into your web browser. He is able to do things such as look up character information, fflogs, equipment, crafting recipes, and much more all from within Discord.

Kupo Bot also handles server moderation with moderator commands, automated spam handling, warnings, mutes, anti-raiding and more.

For Server Owners/Staff

Firstly you will need to invite KupoBot to your server.

To fully utilize KupoBot's powerful staff features the server owner, or someone with manage_guild permissions, will need to configure the required channels/roles. This process is documented in the Configuring Kupobot guide.

Once configuration is complete the following tools will become available:

  • Kupo Bot Staff Commands
    • Users & Channels
      • AutoNick
    • Message/Reaction Purging
    • Roles
      • AutoRoles
    • Notes, Warnings & Strikes
      • Notes
      • Warnings
      • Strikes
    • Spam & Raiding
    • Modmail
    • Reminders
    • Rules
    • Resources
